Welcome to WorkCentre

What is WorkCentre?

WorkCentre is a platform that allows you to create a profile and post your work for others to see. You can also view other people's work and give them feedback. You can also view other people's profiles and see what they have been up to.

How do I get started?

It's easy! Just click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right of the page and fill out the form. Once you have done that, you can start posting your work and viewing other people's work.

How do I post my work?

Once you have signed up, you can click on the "Post" button on the top right of the page and fill out the form. Once you have done that, you can start posting your work and viewing other people's work.

How do I view other people's work?

Once you have signed up, you can click on the "View Work" button on the top right of the page and fill out the form. Once you have done that, you can start posting your work and viewing other people's work.

How do I view other people's profiles?

Once you have signed up, you can click on the "View Profiles" button on the top right of the page and fill out the form. Once you have done that, you can start posting your work and viewing other people's work.

How do I give feedback?

Once you have signed up, you can click on the "Give Feedback" button on the top right of the page and fill out the form. Once you have done that, you can start posting your work and viewing other people's work.
